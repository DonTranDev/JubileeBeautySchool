Our Programs

This course will train students in cutting, styling, chemical services, and salon practices, and provides a basic introduction to manicuring and esthetics.

Students who complete the program and go on to pass the state board's theory and practical exams will be licensed cosmetologists.

• 50 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 34 Weeks – 30 hours per week

1000 Hours

This course will teach students the fundamentals of skin care treatments, facial massage, hair removal and body wraps and body treatments.

Students who complete the program and go on to pass the state board's theory and practical exams will be licensed estheticians.

• 25 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 17 Weeks – 30 hours per week

500 Hours

This course will train students in cutting, styling, chemical services, and salon practices, and provides a basic introduction to esthetics and other salon services.

Students who complete the program and go on to pass the state board's theory and practical exams will be licensed hair designers.

• 35 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 23 Weeks – 30 hours per week

700 Hours

This course will teach students the fundamentals of skin care treatments, facial massage, hair removal, body wraps and treatments.

Students who complete the program and go on to pass the state board's theory and practical exams will be licensed master estheticians.

• 50 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 34 Weeks – 30 hours per week

1200 Hours

This course will train students in basic nail care, manicure, pedicure, acrylic nails, and gel nails with an emphasis on nail art and safety/sanitation procedures.

Students who complete the program and go on to pass the state board's theory and practical exams will be licensed manicurists.

• 20 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 14 Weeks – 30 hours per week

400 Hours

This course will train students in cutting, braiding, shaving, and styling hair.

Students who complete the program and go on to pass the state board's theory and practical exams will be licensed barbers.

• 50 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 34 Weeks – 30 hours per week

About Us

Jubilee Beauty School nurtures talent through hands-on training, small class sizes, and a supportive learning environment. We are committed to preparing our students with the technical skills, professionalism, and confidence needed to thrive in the beauty industry. From cosmetology to esthetics and barbering, our programs are built to launch successful beauty careers.
