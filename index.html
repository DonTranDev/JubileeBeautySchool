Our Programs

(1600 HOURS) This course will train students in cutting, styling, and chemical processing of the hair and provide a basic introduction to manicuring and esthetics. Our objective is to prepare students for an entry-level position in the salon field performing all cosmetology services normally offered in the position

(600 HOURS) This course will train students in nail care with an emphasis on natural nails, artificial nails, manicures, and pedicures and the care of nails and skin that surrounds them. Our objective is to prepare students for an entry-level position in the salon field performing all nail services normally offered in the profession

(750 HOURS) This course will train students in skincare with an emphasis on facials, make-up application, and waxing. Our objective is to prepare students for an entry-level position in the salon field performing all skin services normally offered in the profession

(450 HOURS + completed Esthetician Program) The Master Esthetics program is developed to provide Estheticians with the underpinning knowledge and skills to perform advanced dermal therapies. The program is designed to train the students in the esthetics courses for advanced manipulative skills, safety judgment, proper work habits, business skills, and desirable attitudes necessary to obtain licensure and competency in job entry-level positions in advanced Esthetics or a related career field.

(1000 HOURS) This course will train students in cutting, braiding, shaving, and styling hair. Our objective is to prepare students for an entry-level position in the salon field performing all hair services normally offered in the profession.

(1400 HOURS) This course will train students in cutting, styling, and chemical processing of hair.

(600 HOURS) This course will train instructors in basic instructional methods needed to teach in Cosmetology, Hair Design, Barber, Manicurist, Esthetician, and Master Esthetician programs. The training provided will prepare the graduate with the knowledge and skills needed to pass the State Board Examination and if desired, pursue an entry-level, as an Esthetics, manicurist, Cosmetology, Barber, Hair design, Master Esthetician Instructor, salon manager, and salon owner. Upon completion, the student will obtain a license and be fully qualified to begin working as an entry-level instructor.
