PROGRAM DURATION (1400 Hours)

• 46.75 Weeks – 30 hours per week • 61 Weeks – 23 hours per week

DESCRIPTION: This course will train prospect instructors in basic instructional methods needed to teach in Cosmetology, Hair Design, Barber, Manicure, Esthetics and Master Esthetics.

Note: *The State of Washington requires 500 hours of training for instructor training. Jubilee Beauty School requires an additional 100 hours (600 total hours). These additional hours are used for individualized state board preparations as well as specific hands-on experiences to ensure those in the program are best prepared for the industry.

OBJECTIVE: Students who complete the program and go on to pass the state board's both theory and practical exams will be licensed to work as a Cosmetology Instructor. The objective of this program and curriculum is to prepare the students to become great Cosmetologists, Hair Designers, Barbers, Manicurists, and Estheticians. It is also our mission to develop effective communication skills to connect with future pros from different generations, as well as learning styles and techniques to address different behaviors, evaluate performance, and guide their students to reach their career goals. Upon the completion of six hundred (600) clock hours, a student will obtain a license and be fully qualified to begin working as an entry-level instructor.

INSTRUCTIONAL METHOD: The training provided will prepare the graduates with the knowledge and skills needed to pass the State Board Examination, and if desired, pursue an entry-level position as Estheticians, Manicurists, Cosmetologists, Barbers, Hair Designers/Stylists, (Master) Estheticians, Salon Managers, and Salon Owners.

TEXTBOOK: 3rd Edition Master Educator by Letha Barnes
