Our Programs

This course trains students in cutting, styling, chemical services, and salon practices, and provides a basic introduction to manicuring and esthetics.

Students who complete the program and pass the state board’s theory and practical exams will be licensed cosmetologists.

• 50 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 34 Weeks – 30 hours per week

1000 Hours

This course teaches the fundamentals of skin care treatments, facial massage, hair removal, body wraps, and body treatments.

Students who complete the program and pass the state board’s theory and practical exams will be licensed estheticians.

• 25 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 17 Weeks – 30 hours per week

500 Hours

This course trains students in cutting, styling, chemical services, and salon practices, and provides an introduction to esthetics and other salon services.

Students who complete the program and pass the state board’s exams will be licensed hair designers.

• 35 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 23 Weeks – 30 hours per week

700 Hours

This course teaches advanced skin care treatments, facial massage, hair removal, body wraps, and spa-level services.

Students who complete the program and pass the state board’s exams will be licensed master estheticians.

• 50 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 34 Weeks – 30 hours per week

1200 Hours

This course covers nail care, manicures, pedicures, acrylics, gels, nail art, and sanitation procedures.

Students who complete the program and pass the state board’s exams will be licensed manicurists.

• 20 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 14 Weeks – 30 hours per week

400 Hours

This course trains students in cutting, fades, shaving, grooming, and classic and modern barbering techniques.

Students who complete the program and pass the state board’s exams will be licensed barbers.

• 50 Weeks – 20 hours per week
• 34 Weeks – 30 hours per week

About Us

Jubilee Beauty School nurtures talent through hands-on training, small class sizes, and a supportive learning environment. From cosmetology to esthetics and barbering, our programs are built to launch successful beauty careers.
